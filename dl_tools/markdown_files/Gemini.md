practices:
  - Google reviewers (and service providers) may read, annotate, and process Gemini conversations to improve models.
  - Gemini Apps Activity is turned on by default for users over 18, storing prompts, outputs, and related metadata.
  - Reviewed or annotated conversations may still be stored for up to 3 years even if users delete their history.
  - Google collects IP address, device-level location, and linked Google Account locations to personalize and improve responses.
  - Gemini shares and receives information from other Google apps (e.g., Assistant, Drive) under broad cross-product permissions.
data_info:
  - While exact sources are not disclosed, training includes web data and user feedback.
concerning_practices:
  - Reviewers may access conversations unless Gemini Apps Activity is turned off manually.
  - Reviewed/flagged chats are stored for up to 3 years even after user deletes them from history.
  - Gemini collects and uses device-level and account-based location data to personalize interactions.
  - User interactions with Gemini may influence or be influenced by activity in Gmail, Search, Assistant, and other Google tools, creating unclear data boundaries.
